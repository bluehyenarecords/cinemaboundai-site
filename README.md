# 🎬 Cinemabound  
### Turn Books Into Short Videos

Cinemabound is an AI‑driven storytelling tool that transforms any book into a short, cinematic video summary. Upload a book, manuscript, or story, and Cinemabound automatically analyzes the narrative, identifies key moments, and generates a fast, visual, share‑ready video that captures the essence of the story.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Book Upload**  
  Upload any book, manuscript, or text‑based story.

- **AI Story Analysis**  
  Cinemabound identifies themes, characters, plot points, and emotional beats.

- **Cinematic Video Generation**  
  Automatically creates a short video summarizing the story.

- **Creator‑Friendly Output**  
  Perfect for social media, trailers, pitches, classrooms, and creative projects.

- **Fast + Visual**  
  Converts long text into engaging video clips in minutes.

---

## 🛠 How It Works

1. Upload a book or manuscript  
2. Cinemabound analyzes the story  
3. Key scenes and narrative beats are extracted  
4. A short cinematic video is generated  
5. Download or share instantly
